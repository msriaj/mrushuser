Streamline your distribution process -
use Rush's free & powerful API (Application Programming Interface).

If you have your own website and sell products online, our API (Application Programming Interface) can help you streamline your distribution process saving you both time and money. Rather than typing in the details of each shipment on our website, you can use the API to automatically book your deliveries as soon as a sale is made, with no need for manual entry of data.

Here's how our API can help you:

Save time money

The Rush API eliminates the need to spend hours manually booking collections and deliveries on our website.

Save time money

The Rush API eliminates the need to spend hours manually booking collections and deliveries on our website.

What does it cost?

Using the API is completely free. The only cost involved is that of the deliveries you book at the same price you would can buy on our website. Paying for the deliveries is simple too, all you need to do is top up your prepaid account by logging into your account Rush.co.za

How to get started.

1. Log in to your Rush account or if you don't already have a Rush account, Sign up free here

2. Send an email to our IT Team at itsupport@rush.co.za, with your name, contact details and quoting your registered user email, requesting more information.
